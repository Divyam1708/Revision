complex data type values can't be modulus or floor divided.

Precedence and superiority of data types
1.complex
2.Float
3.int
4,bool

Arithmetic: 
    Addition: 
        int+int=int
        int+float=float
        int+complex(float)=complex(float)
        float+complex=complex
        float+bool=float
        bool+complex=complex
    Substraction: 
        int-int=int
        int-float=float
        int-complex(float)=complex(float)
        float-complex=complex
        float-bool=float
        bool-complex=complex
    Multiplication: 
        int*int=int
        int*float=float
        int*complex(float)=complex(float)
        float*complex=complex
        float*bool=float
        bool*complex(float)=complex
    Division: 
        int/int=float
        int/float=float
        int/complex(float)=complex(float)
        float/complex=complex(float)
        float/bool=float
        bool/complex(float)=complex
    Modulus: 
        int%int=int
        int%float=float
        int%complex(float)=Not moded
        float%complex=Not moded
        float%bool=float
        bool%complex(float)=Not moded
    Exponential: 
        int**int=int
        int**float=float
        int**complex=complex(float)
        float**complex=Complex
        float**bool=float
        bool**complex(float)=complex
    Floor Division: 
        int%int=int
        int%float=float
        int%complex(float)=Not moded
        float%complex=Not moded
        float%bool=float
        bool%complex(float)=Not moded
